\begin{abstract}
We propose the Fractal Substrate Equivalence Physics (FSEP), a geometric framework
in which spacetime is modeled as an infinite recursive degenerate Apollonian sphere
packing of dense and sparse regions. In spacetimes evolving toward $t \to \infty$,
the global domain of validity of the Einstein Equivalence Principle contracts to
measure zero, forcing a breakdown of smooth-manifold descriptions at the
dense--sparse interface. The maximal geometric covering of this interface is an
Apollonian sphere packing, which we take as fundamental rather than emergent.

At each tangency boundary, physical evolution is governed by spherical inversion,
a M\"obius transformation, a discrete scale flip $r \mapsto r/\lambda$ (with
$\lambda \gg 1$), and strict angular-momentum conservation. These rules generate
universal bipolar jets, cross-scale transport, and nonlocal correlations from a
single geometric mechanism, reproducing previously reported statistical results
(Balmer-line clustering and SPARC rotation-curve fits) as coarse-grained
projections of boundary-crossing dynamics.

This paper stands in relation to its predecessor (viXra:2601.0119) as a
foundational extension: where that work derived fractal statistical structure as an
emergent consequence of known physics, the present work takes the fractal as the
primary geometric substrate from which known physics emerges.

FSEP yields four falsifiable predictions: (i) correlated AGN variability across
cosmic voids with lags scaling linearly with void diameter ($\tau \propto
D_{\rm void}$); (ii) systematic dark-matter-fraction depletion in merging galaxy
pairs relative to isolated systems; (iii) jet opening angles directly measuring the
local scale ratio $\lambda_{\rm local}$; and (iv) potential spectral-distortion
signatures in the cosmic microwave background tied to hydrogen recombination
harmonics rather than $\mu$/$y$-type thermal relic distortions.

In this framework, particle-like and black-hole-like structures arise as fractally
coherent boundary configurations rather than fundamental entities. General
Relativity and Quantum Mechanics emerge as effective smooth-manifold approximations
valid away from the fractal boundary.
\end{abstract}

Consider the Schwarzschild--de~Sitter (SdS) spacetime with metric

     + r^2\,d\Omega^2.
\end{equation}
The horizons are roots of $f(r)=1-\tfrac{2GM}{r}-\tfrac{\Lambda r^2}{3}=0$, with
$r_{\rm BH}<r_{\rm c}$ defining the static patch $r_{\rm BH}<r<r_{\rm c}$ where

\subsection{Collapse Regions: Observers Fall to Singularities}
In the SdS static patch, realistic observers experience orbital decay via
gravitational radiation and tidal interactions. All bound trajectories terminate
at the singularity in finite proper time $\tau$, so the physical radial separation
satisfies
\begin{equation}
\lim_{\tau\to\tau_{\rm sing}}\Delta r_{\rm phys}(\tau)=0,\qquad
\Delta r = r_{\rm c}-r_{\rm BH},
\end{equation}

$\Delta r\approx\sqrt{3/\Lambda}-2GM$.
Define the indicator functional:
\begin{equation}

\begin{cases}
1 & \text{if $\mathcal{R}$ contains a finite lab of size $L_{\rm phys}>0$
          sustaining relative motions $>\varepsilon L_{\rm phys}$},\\
0 & \text{if all observers have collapsed
          ($\Delta r_{\rm phys}<\varepsilon L_{\rm phys}$)}.
\end{cases}
\end{equation}
For fixed $\varepsilon>0$ and $L_{\rm phys}>0$, $F_{\rm collapse}(t_{\rm finite})=1$

\begin{equation}
f_{\rm collapse}(t)\sim\frac{[\Delta r(t)]^3}{[\Delta r(0)]^3}\to 0
\quad(t\to\infty).
\end{equation}

The EEP requires finite-size labs capable of controllable internal dynamics. With

$L_{\rm phys}$. The volume fraction of valid EEP labs scales as
\begin{equation}

\end{equation}

This work has proposed a geometric reformulation of fundamental physics based on a
single structural observation: in spacetimes evolving toward $t \to \infty$, the
global domain of validity of the Einstein Equivalence Principle contracts toward a
set of measure zero. If smooth-manifold descriptions fail asymptotically at the
dense--sparse interface, then the boundary structure itself must be taken as
fundamental rather than emergent.

We have argued that the maximal geometric covering of this interface is an infinite
recursive Apollonian sphere packing. Physical evolution across tangency boundaries

transformation, discrete scale flip $r \mapsto r/\lambda$, and strict
angular-momentum conservation. These rules constitute the full dynamical content of

Within this structure, several phenomena commonly treated as independent acquire a
unified geometric origin. Bipolar astrophysical jets follow directly from pole
geometry under inversion. Dark-matter-like rotation profiles arise from sparse-layer
boundary dynamics, with the halo composed predominantly of cold micro-hydrogen
plasma and trace heavier micro-elements at $S{=}{-1}$, shaped primarily by the
orbital pressure of the visible baryonic disk. Cross-void correlations emerge from
ballistic transport at the adjacent scale level. The CMB is the ongoing resonant
eigenmode emission of micro-hydrogen plasma filling every void at every scale below
$S{=}0$, arriving omnidirectionally because we are immersed inside it. The CMB
dipole is the kinematic arrow pointing back to the $S{=}+1$ ejection event that
produced our hydrogen gas clump, and the observed near-isotropic birefringence
$\beta$ is the single M\"obius twist imposed by the one void sphere to which our
dense marble is tangent.

In this framework, particle-like excitations and black-hole-like objects are
interpreted as long-lived fractal boundary configurations rather than fundamental
singular entities. General Relativity and Quantum Mechanics arise as effective
smooth-manifold approximations valid away from the fractal boundary where
scale-averaging restores differentiability.

The theory makes concrete, falsifiable predictions. It is excluded if: correlated
AGN variability fails to scale linearly with void diameter; merging galaxy pairs do
not exhibit systematic halo depletion relative to isolated systems; observed jet
opening angles fail to track local scale ratios; future precision CMB spectral
measurements reveal purely thermal $\mu$/$y$ distortions without harmonic connection
to hydrogen boundary dynamics; the CMB dipole axis proves statistically inconsistent
with any surrounding large-scale overdensity; or $\beta(\hat{n})$ anisotropy is
detected orthogonal to rather than aligned with the dipole axis.

FSEP therefore stands or falls on observational confrontation. The proposal is
geometric at root: the universe is structured by recursive dense--sparse tangencies,
and physical law is the algebra of crossing them.
