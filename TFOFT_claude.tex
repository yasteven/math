\noindent\textbf{Logarithmic step count (Mass)}:

The natural logarithm of the mass ratio from a single electron to a spiral galaxy:

\[
N_{\text{mass}} = \ln\left(\frac{M_{\text{galaxy}}}{m_e}\right)
\]

\noindent Using the Milky Way as reference:
\begin{align*}
M_{\text{MW}} &\approx 1.5 \times 10^{12} M_{\odot} \\
&\approx 3 \times 10^{42} \text{ kg} \\
m_e &\approx 9.109 \times 10^{-31} \text{ kg}
\end{align*}

\[

\subsubsection{Frequency Hierarchy: Electron Orbit to Star Orbit}
